Jinghong(David) He is a sophomore major in Information Systems and Business Analytics and minor in Computer Science at Boston College Wallace E. Carroll School of Management. Graduating from Beijing No.80 High School in May 2018 with Outstanding Graduate Award, he was the President of Botong History Club, Founder and Manager of FC Greater, Vice President of JA Business Club, Chief Director of Coming-of-age Ceremony and Graduation Ceremony, Teaching Assistant of 3D-Printing Program, Editor for School Newspaper, and etc. Being a member of the Wang Xan Innovative and Experimental Course program, he started to code C++ since Sept. 2019. He decided to study abroad after taking the exchange program at Buckswood School, Hastings, UK in early 2016. Growing up in Beijing, but always spending vacations in Shanghai, he likes traveling. He has been to more than 60 cities of 16 different countries in the past 5 years, developing photography and Vlog editting skills.
